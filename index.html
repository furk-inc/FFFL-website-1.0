And So It Begins...

Welcome to the new home of the FFFL

With the jump from free and for fun to paid and for blood the league formerly known as The League 23, The League 27, The G4Y League, and I am sure a few more, is now and henceforth will forever be known as the Furk Fantasy Football League. And in its inaugural namesake year I have thrown together this website as a tribute to the tinkering, the trade raping, and the trash talking that will ensue during what is sure to be an exciting year as we all play for the same CASH prize. Here is to a fun, fair and fully-inflated season of waiver wire thievery, late night rosterbation, and general purpose debauchery!

- Commish
